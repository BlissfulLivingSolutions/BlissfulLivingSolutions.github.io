Privacy Policy

Blissful Living Solutions LLC — Last updated April 2026

Blissful Living Solutions LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website at www.blissfullivingsolutions.com. This page explains how we collect and use information when you visit our site. We provide non-medical home care services only and do not offer or solicit medical services through this website.

Information We Collect

Contact Form

When you submit the contact form on our website, you provide us with:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • A message describing how we can help

Submitting the form opens your device’s default email client and sends your message directly to contact@blissfullivingsolutions.com. We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage data such as which pages are visited, how long visitors stay, and general traffic patterns. This helps us understand how people use the site so we can improve it over time.

Analytics cookies are optional. We do not load any analytics scripts or set any analytics cookies until you explicitly click “Accept Analytics” on the cookie banner shown at your first visit.

If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no usage data is sent to Google.

What Analytics Does Not Collect

Our analytics setup is configured to never receive:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Any text you type into the contact form
  • Precise location data
  • Any information that could identify you individually

We do not use analytics data for advertising. Ad-related Google features (ad storage, ad personalization) remain disabled regardless of your cookie choice.

Third-Party Services

If you accept analytics cookies, data is processed by Google LLC under Google’s Privacy Policy. IP anonymization is enabled. We do not share analytics data with any other third party.

The map embedded on our site is served by OpenStreetMap, which has its own privacy policy.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can change your analytics cookie choice at any time by clicking “Cookie Preferences” in the footer of any page on this site. Choosing “Decline” will prevent any further analytics data from being sent. Your preference is stored locally in your browser and does not expire automatically.

You can also clear your browser’s local storage or cookies at any time to reset your choice.

Data Retention

Contact messages you send us are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and conduct normal business. Anonymous analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default settings (typically 14 months).

Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our practices change. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please email us at contact@blissfullivingsolutions.com.
