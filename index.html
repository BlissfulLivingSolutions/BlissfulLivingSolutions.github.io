Hospice Support

We provide compassionate non-medical support for clients on hospice, coordinating seamlessly with your hospice provider to offer extra comfort, presence, and practical help at home.

Please Note: We are a non-medical agency. We do not provide medical or nursing services such as wound care, injections, medication administration, or tube feeding. We refer to appropriate medical providers when skilled care is needed.

Resources & FAQs

What to Ask Any Home Care Agency

Ask them: Do you check fingerprints and the Health Care Worker Registry?

Blissful Living: Yes. We perform fingerprint-based checks and clear every worker through the IDPH Registry.

Ask them: Is it just a video, or real training?

Blissful Living: 10+ hours initial training before/during the first 30 days, plus 10+ hours annually.

Ask them: How often does a supervisor visit?

Blissful Living: We conduct on-site supervisory visits at least every 90 days.
